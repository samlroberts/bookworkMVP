Find top student talent.

Get matched up with a talented student who meets your needs. Students are vetted through our recommendation process and verify their skills through proficiency tests. Ratings and feedback from students' previous projects will help you get a better sense of their abilities.

Hire the best, before your competitors

No more hiring unknown and untested recent grads. After working with different students for different projects, you’ll quickly see who works best for you. Once these students graduate, you’ll have not only a good sense of their talents, but also if they’d make a good fit for your company.

No hassle payment and processing.

Hiring a student shouldn’t be difficult. We take care of all the paperwork and payment issues. Just agree to an hourly rate or a fixed project cost, and we handle paying the student. All you see is a single invoice.
